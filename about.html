Q. What cameras did you use to take these photos?

A. All of the pictures on this website were taken with the Fuji Cardia Joy Date.

Q. What type of film do you use?

A. Kodak Gold, mostly. (I might have used Ultramax for some of the older photos, but I don't remember).

Q. Where are you from?

A. Portland, Oregon.

Q. How did you get into film photography?

A. I bought my one (and so far only) film camera off of Depop in 2020 during the pandemic. I had used disposable cameras for years before that and thought that it was time to invest in the real thing. I'm glad I did!

Q. What are your favorite places to get film developed?

A. Luster (New York City) and The Shutterbug (Portland, Oregon).

Q. I noticed that there aren't any pictures from 2022 on this website, but the homepage says that this is supposed to chronicle your college years from 2022-2025.

A. Good catch! I didn't bring my camera with me to my first semester of college, so there are no pictures from the Fall 2022 semester.

Q. What is the point of this website?

A. To let me look back on my college years with fondness, I hope.
